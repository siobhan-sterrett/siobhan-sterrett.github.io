You wake up in a white room.

It’s weird, how white it is. You can barely tell that it’s a room – it looks more like a void. You can barely see the edges of the space, the thin outline of a door on the right wall.

The door opens soon after you regain consciousness, and someone walks in – a doctor? A priest?

“Good morning. I imagine you have some questions; please feel free to ask.”

“Where am I? Who are you?”

“You are in a Resurrection Chamber. I am your Mentor.”

You pause, wondering which question you should ask next.
